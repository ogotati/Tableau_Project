# Sales and delivery terms analysis for Superstore

https://public.tableau.com/views/TableauProject-1_17635862308890/DeliveryTermsAnalysis?:language=en-US&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link

Tools: Tableau Public, Excel,  public Dataset Superstore Sales.

Description: study of sales dynamics, analysis of assortment and delivery terms to improve profitability. 

My tasks: built visualizations for the analysis of sales and profit over time, profitability of the assortment in terms of categories and subcategories; interactive dashboard for researching delivery terms by state and type of delivery, developed recommendations for increasing profitability. 

Results: identified unprofitable subcategories of goods, proposed to revise the assortment and price policy to increase the overall profitability of goods.
